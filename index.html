Our little shop history

At one point or another, everyone can say they have experienced a few unforgettable memories in their lifetimes. For me, I’ll never forget about the time I have dressed my dog for the first time and looked at his happy, happy face. I thought I'd like to see more dogs happy. Then this beautiful idea came up. Hope you will enjoy it as much as I do and make your little creatures happy.
